MLDS is the ultimate developers' conference, bringing together India's top innovators and practitioners from across all domains, including AI, Generative AI, and beyond. This event will highlight not only the revolutionary impact of generative AI but also showcase the latest advancements across the entire software development spectrum, from cloud computing and web development to data engineering and DevOps.

MLDS 2025

Attendees will explore emerging trends, cutting-edge tools, and groundbreaking developer products, setting the stage for the next wave of innovation. Whether you're building machine learning models, scaling enterprise applications, or pioneering new technologies, MLDS offers practical insights and hands-on learning experiences.

Join us for deep discussions, hands-on sessions, and networking opportunities that span across every corner of the developer ecosystem, from AI-powered applications to next-gen developer tools. Whether you’re working with AI, software engineering, or something entirely new, this conference is your gateway to the future of development.
