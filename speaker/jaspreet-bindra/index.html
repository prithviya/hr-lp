Jaspreet Bindra

Founder and MD of Tech Whisperer Ltd

Jaspreet Bindra is the Founder and MD of Tech Whisperer Ltd, Cambridge, UK, providing thought leadership and technology expertise around AI, Generative AI, Web3, Future of Work and Digital Transformation.

In his corporate career, Jaspreet was the Group Chief Digital Officer at Mahindra Group, a Regional Director at Microsoft India, and General Manager in the Tata Group, as a part of the select Tata Administrative Services. He was also a member of the Founding Team at Baazee.com, which later became EBAY India

Jaspreet works as a Senior Advisor for ThoughtWorks India, Senior Advisor for PWC, Senior Advisor for Mahindra Holidays and sits on the Advisory Board for Findability Sciences, a Boston- based Enterprise AI firm.

He was recognised as the inaugural Digitalist of the Year by Mint and SAP, teaches at Ashoka University and is an Expert/Faculty for Singularity University and Harvard Business, among others. He is also an accredited Coach (PCC ICF)

Jaspreet is an MBA, a Chemical Engineer, and has a Masters in AI, Ethics and Society at the University of Cambridge.
